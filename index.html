i am hannah

My research revolves around Kata Kolok, a sign language that emerged spontaneously in a village community in Bali. I am interested in the emergence of Kata Kolok and how variation patterns across different signers from the community. I also study how deaf children learn Kata Kolok from an linguistically rich environment.

I am a flexible and creative improviser with a good portion of courage and willingness to take a risk.
My daily work challenges include fieldwork with signing children and adults, spontaneous conversational data and elicited data, as well as an experimental (acquistion) adventure in the field, and language juggling!
